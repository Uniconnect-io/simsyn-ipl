# 1. Executive Summary

The **Simsyn Innovation Premier League (SIPL)** is a 10-month internal
innovation tournament inspired by the structure and excitement of the
IPL.

Six innovation leads build their teams through a live auction using a
virtual token economy. Teams compete head-to-head in idea battles,
advance through playoff rounds, build real POCs, and ultimately develop
production-ready products.

Focus Domains:

-   Customer Engagement AI
-   Contact Center Innovation
-   AI Agents & Voice Automation
-   CX Analytics
-   Workflow Automation
-   AI-driven CRM Enhancements

Objective: Not just ideas --- but commercially viable products built on
Uniconnect.

# 4. Team Formation & Auction

## Team Structure

-   6 Innovation Leads (Captains)
-   Each Lead receives 1,000,000 Virtual Tokens
-   Minimum 5 members + Lead
-   Recommended max: 8 members

## Player Categories & Base Token Values

### Software Engineers

-   Junior -- 80,000
-   Mid -- 120,000
-   Senior -- 180,000
-   Architect -- 250,000

### System Engineers / DevOps

-   Junior -- 100,000
-   Senior -- 180,000

### Project Managers

-   Associate -- 150,000
-   Senior -- 220,000

### AI / Data Specialists

-   250,000 -- 350,000

## Auction Rules

-   Live bidding event
-   Open ascending bid format
-   Highest bid wins
-   Budget deducted from total
-   Teams must meet minimum team size

Optional: - 1 Pre-Auction Retention - 1 Right-to-Match (RTM)

# 6. League Stage -- Head-to-Head Idea Battles

-   6 teams
-   15 total matches
-   Round robin format

Each match includes: - Problem Statement - Target Market - Solution
Architecture - Uniconnect Integration - Revenue Model

Pitch Format: - 15 minutes presentation - 10 minutes Q&A

Scoring (100 Points):

  Criteria                Points
  ----------------------- --------
  Innovation              20
  Market Potential        20
  Technical Feasibility   20
  Uniconnect Alignment    20
  Clarity & Pitch         20

Winner: 2 Points\
Tie: 1 Point Each

Top 4 advance to Playoffs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Playoffs -- POC Championship

Scoring:

  Criteria            Points

  Technical Depth     20

  Business Case       20

Top 2 move to Grand Final.
